Meet Zoey, A game ready character I made for my ‘Stylized Creation’ course at DAE. it’s an original concept I made for another course where we have to make a game in a group, and this will be the character. Exploring the texturing took some time but the biggest hurdle was the posing, and you will probably be able to find some strange artifact cause of that.

  • Modeling: Blender | Zbrush
  • Retopo / unwrap : Blender
  • Texturing : Substance Painter
  • Posing: AccuRIG to Blender (first time)
